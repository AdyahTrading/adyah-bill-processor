Step 1 — Scan vendor bill

Capture or upload the full vendor bill (PDF, JPG, or PNG). The warehouse PC reads it with vision to extract line items, taxes and totals.

Step 2 — Scan products

Scan the barcode with the Bluetooth scanner, then capture one photo of the label showing batch number, manufacturing / expiry date, and MRP. Enter the received quantity. Repeat for every item on the bill.
